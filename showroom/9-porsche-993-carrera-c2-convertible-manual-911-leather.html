We at Karlsen Auto Design are very excited to bring this our extremely rare Porsche 993 C2 Manual Convertible 3.6L onto the International retail Market.

This Porsche has a complete and comprehensive Service portfolio from it's original Service books. The Service book reads like a who is who of Porsche main dealerships including well known Independents. OCD Porsche have recently carried out £1800 woth of work including Alternator, Leads, all documented. 

We previously sourced this vehicle for a fastidious enthusiast client. Since then he has had a Full Leather re-upholstery in keeping with the Porsche originality. Further works have also been undertaken which is all documented in it's time with him. 

Our 993 comes complete with both sets of Porsche keys including alarm remote. AC, 12v Socket, Alpine stereo, Electric Heated Sports seats, Electronically adjustable rear spoiler (new motor by OCD Porsche) Electric windows, Electric mirrors, Tool kit, Jack, Graduated windscreen, Porsche Immobiliser system. Recent new Mohair Electric hood which operates smooth and swift.

The 993 sits on it's original Carrera 2 17" Alloy wheels with new Pirelli Zero Tyres all round.

Further services we offer are Finance options, Part exchange and UK Delivery. (Worldwide delivery Quotes upon request) All vehicles come complete with a full History check (HPi report) to ensure of clean providence and added peace of mind.

Viewing can be arranged any time. Out of hours viewing available by request. We have created a PDF copy of all receipts/service records should any prospective buyer wish to see our vehicles provenance. This has to be the perfect vehicle available in the most rare colour combination with a very high specification.

Viewing highly recommended.
