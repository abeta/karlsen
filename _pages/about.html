Karlsen Auto Design has built its reputation upon organic growth. We have become the UK's leading authority on Supercars and many specialist vehicles.

Our vast network of contacts and industry knowledge has come from our founder Mr Fraser Karlsen, who's experience of all things vehicle related - be it Financing, Buying, Selling or Sourcing is second to none.

In 2010, Fraser saw how the vehicle industry was not utilising the internet in an effective manner. Although it was accessible, he found it to be quite stagnant. He then set I.T. Developers to task developing Karlsen Auto Design a system that puts them ahead of the competition.

Karlsen Auto Design created a route to market for specialist, performance and highly collectable vehicles, including Investment vehicles to be sold effectively worldwide.

With our now unprecedented targeted software, Karlsen Auto Design actively link UK and international buyers with Financiers, Investment portfolios, bonded warehouses, shipping services and Insurance.
