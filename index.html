Welcome to Karlsen Auto Design

Karlsen Auto Design operate throughout the United Kingdom and Internationally. We specialise in Luxury Sports and Prestige Vehicles. Karlsen Auto Design are passionate about delivering unparalled service and expert knowledge to our clients.

As we have an unrivalled knowledge of the market regarding Sports, Performance, Modern and Classic cars. This enables us to buy or source any motor car that you may be looking for or wanting to sell.

Our purpose built software can pin point vehicles all over the UK and International sellers. Karlsen Auto Design are first off the mark regarding purchasing the finest Automobiles. Equally we are keen to hear from sellers of vehicles with excellent provenance, similar to the type we sell.

We are passionate about cars and are committed to the restoration and sale of important classic motor cars.

Karlsen Auto Design have a number of interesting projects which are at various stages of restoration. We are proud to be bringing some of the very best restorations to the market via ourselves and our restoration team.
